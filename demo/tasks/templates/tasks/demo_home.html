Events

The navbar shows a loading spinner during morph fetches. These events power it:

EventWhenUsed For
django-morph:fetch-startFetch beginsShow loading indicator
django-morph:fetch-endFetch completesHide loading indicator
django-morph:updatedDOM patchedRe-init third-party JS
django-morph:errorFetch failsShow error feedback
